5. Data Deletion Instructions

In compliance with Facebook Platform Policy and GDPR, users have the right to request the deletion of their data. However, since online_cinma_bot does not store any user data, there is technically no data to delete from our servers.

If you wish to remove the application and its permissions from your Facebook account, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Facebook account.
  2. Go to your Settings & Privacy > Settings.
  3. Scroll down to find Apps and Websites.
  4. Find online_cinma_bot in the list of active apps.
  5. Click the Remove button next to the app name.
  6. Confirm the removal.

Once removed, the app will no longer have access to your Facebook account.
